Lasagna

Lasagna is the perfect meal. It has all the carbs for energy, all the protein for them biceps and more importantly, it is DELICIOUS. sure, eating more than a few portions will force you into a garfield cosplay; but that's the case with any meal, really. below you can learn how to make this divine goodness!

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Meat sauce(ragu)
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • ricotta

  1. Make the meat ragu

    You could simply buy a can of meat ragu and it would be perfectly fine. however, if you wish to make your own, u'd need the following ingredients:

    • Minced Meat
    • Onions
    • Tomatoes
    • Garlic
    • Black pepper

    Pan-fry your onions after cutting them into slices until they are slightly translucent. add in your meat and cook and stir until it's a shade of gray. now you can mince your garlic and tomatoes and add them to your meat. add some salt and black pepper while you're at it! it's ready when it smells nice and garlicey. add your sliced mushrooms near the end.

  2. Boil your Lasagna sheets in boiling water for 10 minutes

  3. Layering your Lasagna

    cover the bottom of your glass tray or metal pot with lasagna sheets. place enough meat sauce on top to cover the sheets. then cover with another layer of sheets and continue. the last layer of lasagna sheets should be covered with a small amount of meat sauce and a generous portion of mozarella and ricotta cheese.

  4. Bake!

    Place your Lasagna in an oven or on a stove top depending on your choice of container. the timing depends, but enough to melt all the cheese is enough.

  5. Enjoy!
